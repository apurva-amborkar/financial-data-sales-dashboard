# Financial Data Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes financial performance across products, segments, and time periods. It provides actionable insights into sales, profitability, order distribution, and seasonal trends for strategic business decision-making.

---

## Objective
To build an interactive financial analytics dashboard that helps stakeholders:
- Track key performance indicators (Sales, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin)
- Analyze product-wise and segment-wise performance
- Identify seasonal sales trends
- Evaluate profitability across markets

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following core metrics:

- **Total Sales:** 118.73M  
- **Total Orders:** 1.13M  
- **Total Profit:** 16.89M  
- **Profit Margin:** 14.23%  
- **PreMTD:** 5.38M  

---

## Visualizations & Insights

### Product Performance
- **Orders by Product:** Paseo leads with 338K orders, while Carretera records the lowest at 147K  
- **Top Revenue Products:** Paseo (33M), VTT (21M), and Velo (18M) are top contributors  
- **Product Matrix:** Detailed breakdown of sales, profit, and margin across all products  

---

### Market & Segment Analysis
- **Discount Distribution:** High and Medium discount bands account for ~70% of total transactions  
- **Segment Profitability:** Government segment leads with 11M profit, while Enterprise shows a loss (-1M)  
- **Customer Segments:** Clear variation in profitability across market categories  

---

### Sales Trend Analysis
- Monthly sales remain stable between 6M–10M in early months  
- Significant spike observed in **October (22M)** and **December (17M)**  
- Clear seasonal pattern in revenue performance
